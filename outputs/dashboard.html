the gap

Aesthetic language — cottagecore, coquette, gorpcore — is how people describe themselves. But the same people, in their Amazon reviews, reach for fit, size, comfortable, quality.

The story we tell about our style and the basket we actually fill barely overlap. Aesthetic identity lives on Tumblr and Pinterest; the checkout is purely functional.
